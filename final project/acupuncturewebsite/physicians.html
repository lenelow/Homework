Amy Kossoff, MD, LAc

Dr. Amy Kossoff graduated from The George Washington School of Medicine in 1983 and has been practicing Internal Medicine for the underserved for the past 30 years. In October 2017 she completed a fellowship in Integrative Medicine through the University of Arizona Center for Integrative Medicine. She went on to study hypnosis through the American Society of Clinical Hypnosis and become a licensed Acupuncturist through the Helms Medical Institute. She is the Medical Director of Access to Integrative Medicine, a non-profit dedicated to providing Integrative Medical care to underserved populations in Washington, D.C.

Mary Warfield, MD, LAc

Dr. Mary Warfield is a Board Certified Internist and a licensed Acupuncturist. After graduating from Georgetown Medical School she completed her internship and residency at the Washington Hospital Center. Her 30+ years of experience in healthcare includes experience in a variety of medical settings, such as: teaching and supervising medical students, interns and residents as an associate professor for the Georgetown Department of Medicine at District of Columbia General Hospital, practicing internal medicine at Washington Hospital Center, and as clinical director of Student Health Services at American University.
