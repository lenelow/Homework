At the Center for Integrative Acupuncture, we offer holistic treatment plans that incorporate all aspects of our patients' health. Our training in Western Medicine, Complimentary Medicine, Acupuncture, Hypnosis and beyond allow us to offer a variety of treatments for almost any medical condition. Using both individual and group visits, we're here to give you the skills and support you need to lead healthy and productive lives.

Our Services

  • Integrative consultation: Explore all aspects of your physical, mental, emotional and spiritual health.
  • Acupuncture: We believe that everyone can benefit from acupuncture, whether for energetic enhancement or to treat a multitude of ailments, including chronic pain. Sessions are offered with or without additional integrative consultation.
  • Hypnosis: Hypnosis is helpful for many conditions, including but not limited to insomnia, pain, anxiety and depression. Sessions are offered with or without additional integrative consultation.
  • Pain management: We are particularly interested in using Integrative techniques to manage pain, particularly chronic pain. We offer a personalized combination of modalities tailored to your needs. Additionally, we offer group visits for patient education and support.
